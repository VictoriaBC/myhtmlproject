Art

Have had love for art since I could remember. Drawing is really part of my daily life, just i different forms, whether it be for UX work or just doodles. I pretty much like drawing illustrations, but also painting. Some of my Illustrations were presented at Friskis & Svettis in Sweden and also in Uppsala Bio's yearly report. My illustrations can be found also in Addera.

In terms of painting my main instrument is guache and oil paints. Since a child I have loved art history and always found Flemish art fascinating. That's also why I love naturemorte/still life painting.
