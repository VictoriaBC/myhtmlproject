Photography

My passion for photographywas born back in 2008. I then started taking travel photos with my Nikon Coolpix 100. This was also my digital camera. At the time I mostly interested in architecture, landscape and art. It was not until 2009 i decided to be more seriuos with photography and by 2010 set a goal to snap 100 headshots. Achieved that within a year and that is how I ended up earning on photography.

As an artist I totally prefer creative photography and always follow emotions, form and color. For this reason most of my shots are not very tradtional. And did I mention I am fluent in Photoshop? Well, taking shots is one thing, but I also enjoy using my fantasy and retouching photos.

My main tools apart from my camera is Adobe Photoshop and Lightroom. I work with analog and digital cameras so you can say I sometimes use the darkroom.
