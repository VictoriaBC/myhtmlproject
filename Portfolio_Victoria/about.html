About me

I am a Consultant Art Director/UI&UX Designer. As a person I am creative, curious and a problem solver. I like to act when seeing the need and importance of leadership, that’s why I am always ready to lead the team. I have a number of years of experience in visual design. I have been working with User Experience and User Interface Design for over 5 years. I have helped well-known organizations such as Apoteket, Gunnebo, ABB and Bombardier in Sweden.
